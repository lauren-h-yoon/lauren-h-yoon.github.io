Escaping Plato's Cave: JAM for Aligning Independently Trained Vision and Language Models
PMLR / NeurIPS 2025 (UniReps)
Aligns frozen, independently trained vision and language backbones via small autoencoders meeting at a shared bottleneck. A novel Spread Loss turns the Platonic Representation Hypothesis into a training objective — matching a fine-tuned CLIP at ~1/3 of the FLOPs. Google DeepMind; with Been Kim & Yisong Yue.

2026 · under submission
Agents stay generic and disposable; the only thing that persists is a shared, curated knowledge base. Transient agents attempt tasks and debate across them, and what survives is distilled into reusable bundles — higher solve rates at lower cost, transferring zero-shot across model families.

VendiEvolve: Towards Diversity-Aware LLM-Guided Evolutionary Search
ICML 2026 (Human-AI Co-Creativity)
LLM-guided evolutionary search collapses onto a single program family. VendiEvolve puts the Vendi Score into the loop as a control signal, steering selection toward diversity so the search keeps its stepping-stones to better solutions.
