I engineer foundation models to be **stably, efficiently stateless** — carrying no frozen state of their own, but constantly augmenting knowledge and therefore constantly improving. Two questions drive my research: how to align representations that were formed independently into one shared space, and how to let a model actively learn as the knowledge compounds through interaction. As a 2nd year PhD student, I'm advised by [Yisong Yue](http://www.yisongyue.com/).

My fundamental motivation for research stems from philosophy — *National Representative of South Korea to the International Philosophy Olympiad (2019)* — focusing on philosophy of mind and epistemology. This paved my initial path into computational neuroscience.
