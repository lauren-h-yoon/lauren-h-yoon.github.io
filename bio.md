I work on **efficient, stateful foundation models**. Two questions drive my research: how to align representations that were formed independently into one shared space, and how to let a model direct its own test-time compute so that reasoning and knowledge accumulate instead of evaporating.

Before Caltech I spent several years in computational neuroscience, aligning neural activity with behavior — the same problem, in biology. I'm advised by [Yisong Yue](http://www.yisongyue.com/).
