I work on efficient engineering of **stateful** foundation models to be **stateless**. Two questions drive my research: how to align representations that were formed independently into one shared space, and how to let a model actively learn as the knowledge compounds through interaction. As a 2nd year PhD student, I'm advised by [Yisong Yue](http://www.yisongyue.com/).

My direction is deeply inspired by my experience in computational neuroscience, aligning neural activity with behavior or psychological motifs.
